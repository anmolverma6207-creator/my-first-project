About Us

Built for the People of Shamsabad

Sarvan Cyber Cafe was started by Sarvan Singh in Toola Tivriya, Shamsabad with one goal — bring every government and digital service within walking distance of home. No more long trips to Agra city for a PAN card or a railway ticket.

Seven years on, we're still the same: honest pricing, patient guidance, and a fully equipped desk that handles everything from Aadhaar updates to GST filing.
